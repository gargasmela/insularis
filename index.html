About

The power of four different cultures mixed and mastered in Amsterdam! Come taste it!

Insularis is a metal band forged in the underground. Our sound draws from the weight of doom, the edge of thrash, and the atmosphere of black metal—delivered live with full intensity.

We believe in writing music that stands the test of time: heavy riffs, crushing dynamics, and lyrics that cut deep. No gimmicks. No compromise. Just the kind of metal that hits you in the chest and stays there.

From small clubs to bigger stages, we bring the same energy every night. If you're into bands that still care about tone, groove, and presence, you're in the right place.
